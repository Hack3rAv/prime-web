SERVER RULES

These rules are mandatory for all players. Ignorance of the rules is not an excuse.

GENERAL ROLEPLAY RULES

1.1 Stay In Character

CORE

You must remain in character at all times. Out-of-character communication in voice or local chat is strictly prohibited.

1.2 Powergaming (PG)

PUNISHABLE

Performing unrealistic actions or forcing roleplay on others without allowing a response.

1.3 Metagaming (MG)

STRICT

Using out-of-character information in-character is strictly forbidden.

COMBAT RULES

2.1 Deathmatching (DM)

ZERO TOLERANCE

Killing another player without valid roleplay is strictly forbidden.

2.2 New Life Rule (NLR)

IMPORTANT

After death, you must forget all events leading to the situation and may not return for a set time.

ADMINISTRATION & ENFORCEMENT

3.1 Admin Decisions

FINAL

Admin decisions are final. Disrespecting admins or arguing punishments in public channels is prohibited.

3.2 Abuse Reports

PROCEDURAL

Abuse reports must be filed through the official appeal system. Public accusations are forbidden.

⚠️ RULE VIOLATIONS HAVE CONSEQUENCES

Punishments may include warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity and repetition.
