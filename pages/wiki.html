1. GENERAL ROLEPLAY RULES

1.1 Stay In Character (IC)

CORE

You must remain in character at all times. Using "My mic is broken" or talking about your real-life day in voice chat is FailRP.

1.2 Powergaming

BANABLE

Performing impossible acts or forcing actions on others (e.g. "Smashes your head so you die instantly").

2. COMBAT RULES

2.1 RDM (Random Deathmatch)

Killing someone without any prior verbal interaction or roleplay reason is strictly forbidden.

2.2 VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)

Using your vehicle as a weapon to ram or kill players is not allowed.

3. RP VIOLATIONS

3.1 FearRP

You must show fear for your life. If someone has a gun to your head, you cannot just run away or pull out your own gun.

4. ADMINISTRATION

Staff Assistance

Use /report to contact staff. Do not spam. Provide clips/recordings for proof.

5. JOBS & ECONOMY

Legal: Delivery & Mining

Earn clean money at the Job Center. These jobs are safe from police interference.

Illegal: Drug Crafting

High reward but high risk. Police can arrest you and seize your items if caught.
